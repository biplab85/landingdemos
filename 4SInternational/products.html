Our Products

We carry the full range of anti-friction bearings classified under HS 8482. All products are sourced from ISO 9001 certified manufacturers. If you have a branded part number (SKF, NTN, Timken, Schaeffler, NSK), we can cross-reference it to a compatible equivalent.

Deep Groove Ball Bearings

HS Code: 8482.10 | Canadian MFN Duty: Free

The most widely used bearing type in industrial applications. Available in single-row and double-row configurations, sealed (2RS) and shielded (ZZ), in metric and imperial bore sizes from 10mm to 200mm+.

Common applications: Electric motors, pumps, conveyors, fans, HVAC systems, compressors, general-purpose machinery.

Popular specifications: 6200 series, 6300 series, 6000 series, 6800 series.
