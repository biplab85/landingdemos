What We Do

4S International is a Mississauga-based bearing importer and wholesale distributor. We supply anti-friction bearings classified under HS 8482 to small and mid-size industrial businesses across Canada.

We source from manufacturers in China, Japan, India, Thailand, and Vietnam.
