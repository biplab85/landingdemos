About4S International

01

4S International Inc. is a federally incorporated Canadian bearing importer and distributor based in Mississauga, Ontario. We supply anti-friction bearings to industrial businesses across Ontario and Canada.

02

We operate from a 2,000 sq ft warehouse and office at 3400 Ridgeway Drive, in the Pearson Airport industrial corridor with direct access to Highway 401, Highway 403, and Toronto Pearson International Airport.

03

Our product range covers the full HS 8482 classification: ball bearings, roller bearings, thrust bearings, and bearing accessories. All products come from ISO 9001 certified manufacturers across five countries. Every shipment includes certificates of conformity, material test reports, and dimensional inspection records.

04

We serve machine shops, food processors, packaging plants, agricultural equipment dealers, maintenance contractors, and regional distributors. Our minimum order is a single case, and we offer flexible payment terms for repeat customers.

Our Founder

Fatema Islam

Founder and General Manager

Ms. Islam has worked in the bearing trade since 2019, operating S.S Bearing and Machineries on Nawabpur Road in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Over six years, she has built sourcing relationships with manufacturers across China, Japan, Thailand, and India, negotiating pricing, coordinating international shipping, and verifying product quality against ABEC and ISO 492 standards.

She holds bearing inventory valued at approximately CAD $185,000 through her existing operations. Her trade credentials are registered with Bangladesh's National Board of Revenue, Dhaka South City Corporation, and VAT authorities.

4S International brings Ms. Islam's sourcing expertise and supplier network to the Canadian market.
