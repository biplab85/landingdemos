Our Services

Pacific Roofing specialises in all aspects of the roofing industry. Our experienced and professional tradesmen provide a comprehensive range of roofing, guttering and downpipe repairs and replacement, as well as the installation of whirlybirds and skylights.

What we do

Roofing, done properly

From a single cracked tile to full gutter replacement — domestic and commercial, no job too big or small.

Roof repairs

Pacific Roofing can replace all cracked and broken tiles. We also inspect all ridge cappings and report if any are loose, broken or in need of rebed and pointing.

Valley replacement

Poorly maintained or rusted valleys are a common cause of roof leaks — leaves bunch together and water backs up into the roof space. We replace all rusted valleys and supply and install new galvanised or Colorbond valleys.

Gutter repairs

Replacing rusting or leaking gutters is a specialty for our experienced tradesmen. We fit new Colorbond gutters and downpipes in a wide range of colours to suit any home — downpipes available in Colorbond or PVC, whichever you prefer.

Insurance & strata maintenance

Pacific Roofing services strata and real-estate managed properties. We understand roofing problems threaten owners' and occupants' lifestyles, so our skilled tradesmen look after them personally as well as technically.

Rebed & re-pointing of ridge cappings

As a roof ages, the cement under the ridge caps deteriorates. We remove the caps completely, bed them on a new layer of cement and re-point with flexible pointing — adding years of life while flexing with the natural movement of the house.

Gutter cleaning

Blocked gutters lead to flooded eaves and ceiling spaces. We clean all gutter types — domestic to commercial — and unblock downpipes. Arrange a 3, 6 or 12-month regular clean so you never worry about overflow, blockages or leaf build-up in bush-fire-prone areas.
