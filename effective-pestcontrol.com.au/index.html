+ Before we arrive

A five-minute
prep checklist.

A quick prep makes the treatment more effective and gets you back into your home faster. Here's what to do before we pull up.

  1. 01

    Vacate during spraying

    Step out for the duration of the spray, then return after about 1–2 hours once the chemical has settled.

  2. 02

    Pull in clothes & towels

    Bring in anything hanging on the line so it doesn't catch overspray during external treatment.

  3. 03

    Clear the skirting

    Move clutter away from skirting boards and inside corners — that's where most of the work happens.

  4. 04

    Keep driveways clear

    We'll need a parking spot close to the property so we're not running cables and gear across the street.

  5. 05

    Watch the weather

    Heavy rain reduces effectiveness of external sprays — we may need to reschedule. We'll always call ahead.
