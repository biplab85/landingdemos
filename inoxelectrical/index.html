About Inox Electrical

Local Sydney experts who get the job done right

Inox Electrical Pty Ltd is a leading electrical service provider based in Box Hill, New South Wales, delivering high-quality solutions to homes and businesses across the Greater Sydney Region. Our experienced team specialises in everything from renovations and fault finding to switchboard upgrades and safety switch installations.

As accredited Master Electricians, we complete every project with precision and to the highest safety and industry standards. We back our work with a Lifetime Workmanship Warranty — so you can be confident the job is done right the first time.
